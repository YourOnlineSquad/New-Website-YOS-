THE BIG TECH NIGHTMARE

Over the last ten years, we have built websites and answered the phone for thousands of local businesses. We have watched professional handyman services bleed cash dealing with faceless monopolies and predatory lead aggregators. It stops here.

The "Handyman" and "Home Services" categories are arguably the most crowded and abused sectors in the digital world. Every guy with a truck and a drill calls himself a handyman, and Big Tech loves the chaos. Google is a trillion-dollar algorithm that will gladly take your daily advertising budget, but the exact second a random automated spam filter decides to flag and suspend your Google Business Profile, they vanish. They do not have call centers. You cannot speak to a human being. They leave your business completely paralyzed while unlicensed low-ballers steal your local visibility.

Because Big Tech completely alienated the skilled tradesman, an entire parasitic industry of scammers has moved in to feast on your frustration. They call your cell phone relentlessly from foreign boiler rooms, pretending to be "Google Maps Support." They tell you that your home service listing is broken, or they threaten to permanently delete your business from the map unless you pay them a $500 "verification fee." When your phone stops ringing and you need to pivot your strategy to target more profitable work like deck repairs or door installations, you are forced to wait on hold for twenty minutes, only to speak with an account manager who doesn't know the difference between a load-bearing stud and a curtain rod.

We built Your Online Squad because we violently reject this entire model. We do not hide behind email templates. We answer the phone. We understand that in the handyman business, you need jobs that actually justify your drive time and tool wear. We fight the algorithms on your behalf, we protect your digital profile from getting hijacked, and we stand in the trenches with you to ensure your digital real estate is actually generating revenue.
