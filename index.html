FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Real answers to the contractor growth questions that matter most.

Why is my Google Business Profile not ranking in the Local Map Pack?

If your profile is missing from top results, the usual causes are weak local relevance, inconsistent business data, and low authority signals from your website. Our synchronization process fixes these issues so your profile can compete where calls are won.

What is E EAT and why does it matter for contractors?

E EAT stands for experience, expertise, authority, and trust. If your site does not prove these signals, rankings stay unstable. We build the pages, schema, and supporting content that verify your credibility.

Is Local SEO better than Local Services Ads?

Ads can drive short term volume, but that channel is rented. Local SEO builds a long term asset that keeps producing qualified calls without paying for every click.

How do speed and mobile UX affect ranking?

Most urgent contractor searches happen on phones. If your site is slow or hard to use, rankings and conversions both decline. We engineer performance first builds that load fast and convert cleanly.

What is technical schema markup?

Schema is structured data that helps search engines read your business details correctly. We implement service, location, and business schema so your offerings and territory are clear.

How do reviews impact local visibility?

Reviews are a core trust signal. Strong review volume, quality language, and recency all improve how your profile performs in local discovery.
