FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Real answers to the most critical local ranking and contractor growth questions.

Why is my Google Business Profile (GBP) not ranking in the Local Map Pack?

Nearly 70% of local contractor calls come from the top 3 spots in the Map Pack. If you aren't there, it is usually due to poor geo-relevance, lack of localized Schema markup on your website, or inconsistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) citations across the web. Our system focuses heavily on Map Pack Synchronization to force your profile into that top 3 radius.

What are Google's E-EAT guidelines, and why do contractors need them?

E-EAT stands for Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness. Google's recent core updates specifically penalize "thin" contractor websites. To rank, your site must technically prove you are a licensed, experienced professional through specific content silos, author bios, project case studies, and advanced local schema code.

Is Local SEO better than paying for Google Local Services Ads (LSAs)?

LSAs are a great short-term strategy, but they are a rented asset. The second you stop paying Google, your leads drop to zero. Local SEO builds "Digital Equity." Once your site and GBP rank organically, you generate continuous, exclusive leads 24/7 without paying per click. The ultimate goal is to transition your budget away from expensive ads and into owned organic traffic.

How does website speed and mobile design affect my contractor ranking?

Over 80% of emergency searches (like "plumber near me") happen on a smartphone. Google uses "Mobile-First Indexing," meaning if your site is slow or hard to use on a phone, Google will refuse to rank you—even if your content is great. We build highly optimized, conversion-focused websites that load instantly on all devices.

What is Technical Schema Markup for local service businesses?

Schema is the hidden code that speaks directly to search engines. For a contractor, we inject LocalBusiness Schema, ServiceArea Schema, and FAQ Schema into your website's backend. This explicitly tells Google exactly what services you offer, your exact service radius, and your credentials, allowing you to bypass competitors who rely purely on text.

How do online reviews impact local search visibility?

Reviews are one of the strongest "Trust" signals in the E-EAT algorithm. However, it's not just about the star rating; Google scans the actual text of your reviews for keywords. A review that says "Great roof replacement in Santa Ana" is mathematically worth more than a review that just says "Good job." We help implement review generation strategies to build velocity.

How is "Digital Equity" different from buying HomeAdvisor leads?

When you buy leads from directories, you are renting access. Those platforms sell the exact same homeowner's information to 4 or 5 other contractors, forcing a bidding war. Digital Equity means we build the website and organic ranking structure so the traffic comes directly to your brand. The leads generated are 100% exclusive to your business.
