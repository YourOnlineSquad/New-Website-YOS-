FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Honest answers for business owners who want to know how the process works.

How long does SEO take to work?

Search engine optimization is a long term strategy. While we often see initial improvements in the first few months, real and consistent momentum usually builds between months three and six.

Can you work on my current website?

Yes. We can work with almost any major platform including WordPress, Wix, Square, and Shopify. We only recommend a rebuild if your current site is severely broken.

What if I already worked with another SEO company?

Many of our best clients came to us after a bad experience. We will audit the previous work, keep what is helping you, and fix whatever was done incorrectly.

Why does switching SEO companies hurt momentum?

Search engines reward stability. Constantly changing your strategy, tearing down pages, and rebuilding your foundation prevents you from ever building real authority.

What makes monthly SEO different?

The internet is constantly changing. Competitors are always trying to outrank you. Monthly SEO ensures we are constantly expanding your reach and defending your top positions.

Do I own the website and assets?

Absolutely. We build digital assets that you own entirely. We do not hold domains hostage or trap you in proprietary software contracts.

Why does commitment matter?

Growth requires partnership. We commit our resources to dominating your local market, and we need clients who are equally committed to the process and ready to handle the increased call volume.
