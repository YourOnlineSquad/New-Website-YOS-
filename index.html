FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How is "Digital Equity" different from buying leads?

When you buy leads from platforms like HomeAdvisor or Angie's, you are renting access to their traffic. The moment you stop paying, your phone stops ringing. Furthermore, those platforms sell the exact same homeowner's information to multiple contractors, forcing you to lower your prices to win the bid. Digital Equity means we build the website, the Google Business Profile, and the organic ranking structure so that the traffic comes directly to you. You own the asset. The leads are 100% exclusive.

Why do you require Territory Exclusivity?

We operate as your outsourced growth department. It is a fundamental conflict of interest for us to rank two competing roofers or plumbers in the exact same city. If we take you on as a client, we lock down your zip codes. We will not take on another contractor in your specific vertical within your service area. This guarantees that all of our resources, backlink building, and conversion optimization are completely dedicated to making you the undisputed market leader.

How long does it take to see Lead Velocity?

While organic E-EAT SEO is a long-term play that compounds over 6 to 12 months, our Squad Engines are built for immediate velocity. By utilizing Map Pack synchronization, localized service ads, and CRM automation, we typically begin seeing qualified, exclusive lead flow within the first 30 to 45 days. The ultimate goal is to transition your primary revenue source from paid acquisition to organic dominance, drastically reducing your Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) over time.
