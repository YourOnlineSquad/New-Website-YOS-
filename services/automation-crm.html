AUTOMATION FAQS

Direct answers about how we build your mechanical sales pipeline.

I am not tech-savvy. Will this CRM be too hard to use?

No. We built this specifically for guys in trucks, not guys in cubicles. We handle all the complex technical setup, the API connections, and the automation writing. All you have to do is log into an app on your phone and reply to the people who want to buy from you.

Do automated texts sound like a robot?

Only if they are written poorly. We engineer our initial outreach scripts to sound highly conversational and hyper-local. The goal is to get the homeowner to reply to the text so you or your dispatcher can take over and close the deal like a normal human being.

Can this replace my current dispatching software?

Our CRM is designed for sales and marketing capture, not dispatching. However, we integrate our systems seamlessly with industry-standard platforms like ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, and Jobber, so your leads flow perfectly from initial click to final invoice.

What happens if a customer calls instead of texts?

We provide you with dynamic tracking numbers that forward directly to your cell phone or office line. If you miss the call because you are under a house or on a roof, our system instantly detects the missed call and automatically texts the customer back: "Hey, sorry I missed you, I'm on a job. How can I help?"

Will sending mass texts get me marked as spam?

We strictly adhere to A2P 10DLC compliance laws for business texting. We register your business properly with the telecom carriers and ensure all outbound automation includes proper opt-out language to protect your digital reputation and keep your delivery rates at 99%.

How fast can we deploy this system?

Once we have access to your existing customer lists and current lead sources, we can generally build out your pipelines, write the custom nurture sequences, and have your automated system live and capturing leads within two weeks.
