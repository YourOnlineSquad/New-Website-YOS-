HOW WE FILTER THE
NOISE

Volume means absolutely nothing if the quality is garbage. We engineer systems that automatically filter out bad fits before your phone ever rings.

Friction Based Forms

We do not want every single person to fill out a contact form. We intentionally design our web forms to ask qualifying questions. We ask about project timelines, budgets, and specific service needs. This immediately eliminates people who are just looking for cheap estimates.

Dynamic Call Tracking

We assign specific tracking phone numbers to your landing pages and ad campaigns. Every time a prospect calls, the software records the source and the duration of the conversation. This proves exactly which campaigns are generating real revenue.

Direct CRM Integration

Speed is the ultimate sales tool. Our systems connect directly to your customer database software. When a verified homeowner submits a request, your team receives an instant notification with all the project details laid out. You call them back while they are still holding their phone.

LEAD GENERATION FAQS

Direct answers about how we manage ads and build your sales pipeline.

Do you sell leads to multiple contractors at once?

Absolutely not. We operate on a strict exclusivity model. Any phone call or project request generated through the digital assets or landing pages we manage belongs solely to your company. We refuse to manufacture bidding wars for our clients.

What is the difference between a website and a sales funnel?

A website is a digital brochure that explains everything your company does. A sales funnel is a specialized path designed to do exactly one thing. It captures a visitor and forces them to make a decision. By removing navigational menus and focusing purely on the offer, our landing pages convert traffic into paying customers at a significantly higher rate than standard web design.

How much should a contractor spend on Google Ads?

Your advertising budget should always be dictated by your cost per acquisition. We start with a highly targeted budget to gather data. Once we prove that we can acquire a plumbing or roofing lead for a profitable margin, the only logical step is to scale that budget indefinitely. If you put one dollar into the machine and get five dollars back, you do not cap the budget.

What is a Cost Per Acquisition and why does it matter?

Cost Per Acquisition is the exact amount of money you spend to acquire one paying customer. Unlike vanity metrics like clicks or impressions, this is the only math that matters for scaling. Our entire system is designed to lower your acquisition costs over time by optimizing your sales funnels and refining your ad campaigns.

How do you prevent spam calls and fake form submissions?

We implement multiple layers of security on every landing page. We use advanced recaptcha systems, block known spam internet addresses, and design our contact forms to require specific project details. This drastically reduces junk traffic and ensures your sales team only speaks to legitimate homeowners.

Can we choose which specific services we want leads for?

Yes. If your roofing company makes the most profit on full replacements and loses money on minor patching repairs, we adjust the ad spend immediately. We aggressively target the replacement keywords and build specific landing pages just for that service. We structure your entire digital footprint to attract your most lucrative customer profiles.
