LEAD GENERATION FAQS

Direct answers about how we build your sales pipeline.

Do you sell leads to multiple contractors at once?

Absolutely not. We operate on a strict exclusivity model. Any phone call or project request generated through the digital assets we manage belongs solely to your company. We refuse to manufacture bidding wars for our clients.

How do you prevent spam calls and fake form submissions?

We implement multiple layers of security. We use advanced recaptcha systems, block known spam IP addresses, and design our contact forms to require specific project details. This drastically reduces junk traffic and ensures your sales team only speaks to legitimate homeowners.

What is a Cost Per Acquisition and why does it matter?

Cost Per Acquisition is the exact amount of money you spend to acquire one paying customer. Unlike vanity metrics like clicks or impressions, this is the only math that matters for scaling. Our entire system is designed to lower your acquisition costs over time by building highly efficient organic search assets.

Can we choose which specific services we want leads for?

Yes. If your roofing company makes the most profit on full replacements and loses money on minor patching repairs, we aggressively target the replacement keywords. We structure your entire digital footprint to attract your most lucrative customer profiles.

How do I know if the calls are actually coming from your system?

We use dynamic call tracking software. We assign specific tracking phone numbers to your website and your Google Business Profile. Every time a prospect calls, the software records the source, the duration, and even the audio of the conversation. You get a transparent report every month.

Why is organic traffic better than paid advertising leads?

Paid ads are expensive and fleeting. The moment you stop paying, the phone stops ringing. Organic search traffic comes from people actively researching solutions to their problems. Because organic results are highly trusted by consumers, these leads close at a much higher rate and cost significantly less over the lifetime of your business.
